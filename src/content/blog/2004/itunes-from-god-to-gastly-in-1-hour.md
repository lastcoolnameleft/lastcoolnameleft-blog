Here's my progression:

Breakup -> Blues
Went shopping and found 2 DVD's of [Happy Tree Friends](http://www.happytreefriends.com) and cool new pants and shoe rack that I've been needings for about 2 years -> Happy
Got home and listened to Blues -> Blues
Put together shoe rack -> Productive
Watched Happy Tree Friends DVD -> Amused
Found KICK ASS "Break Up Songs" compilation on iTunes, which really hits the spot -> Elated!
Problem downloading songs via iTunes, chatted with friends -> Happy
Finally downloaded files with iTunes.  Some files missing -> Irked
[Found out iTunes won't let me re-download music I've purchased.](http://www.linkstew.org/noid/1910.html) (Even when their software failed the original download.) -> PISSED!
