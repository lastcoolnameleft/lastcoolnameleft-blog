Within the last 6 hours of my stint in Portland, I'm in the back seat of Barb's (who must have cast some type of spell on my Lee to make him so smitten by her), almost drooling on her pillow (containing a patter that strangely resembles a part of the female anatomy) and I then wake up to the Pacific Ocean.  Not just any part of it.  Cannon Beach..."Astoria."...Where they shot "[Goonies](http://www.thegoonies.com/)".

"Holy cow!  That's..."
"Yeah."
"Sweet!"
"Dude."

Having never woken up to [such a majestic site](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album82&id=IMG_0394&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) in my life, [I started frolicking.](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album82&id=IMG_0352&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php).  I had no idea that this was what my soul needed.  Upteen billion gallons of water.  And [a rock](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album82&id=IMG_0332&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) sticking out of it.

Thanks [Barb and Lee](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album83&id=pano29&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php), for the grand-times.  Oh yeah, and for letting me play with your iPod.  I'm sure eventually, I'll learn to use it properly.

And a special thank you to [Heidi](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album82&id=IMG_0207&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php).  I was never a big fan of sushi.  (Now I know it's about as good of a laughing instrument as milk.)  Oh yeah, and for the Architecture geek-out time too.
