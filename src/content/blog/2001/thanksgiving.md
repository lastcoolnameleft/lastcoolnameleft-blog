As
the previous entries describe, last weekend, I spent it in Interlaken (which
means between lakes), Switzerland which is in the middle of the Swiss Alps.

After getting enough courage to do a trip by myself and hearing enough
about Interlaken I bought tickets for my humble journey to Switzerland. 
On the way to the train, I was stopped by two American girls who were also
on their way from Paris to Interlaken and had missed their two previous trains
and wanted me to take a picture of them in front of the train as proof. 
I was quite amused.  So, I took their picture and went on my way.  After
various layovers, I got off the train and into Interlaken only to realize,
"I haven't booked a hotel."  Luckily, I found the same two girls and asked
if I could bum a ride with them to whatever hotel they were going to.  I
later found out that they were going to a hostel instead.  (Quick note: 
Hostels are usually for college aged kids that are backpacking all over Europe
can stay for CHEAP prices  $16-20/night in a dorm type room.)  So, around
midnight on Friday night, [Balmer's ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=58839436203)became my new home for the next two days.

I walked inside and was warmly greeted by an Astralian girl who had check in
soon before me and was sitting down with a bunch of other college kids in
front of the fireplace drinking beer.  "Quite a cozy atmosphere," I thought. 
I then went about the check in procedure and opted for the single room since
I figured that my budget allowed for it. :)  40 CHF/night  (~$20)  I went
up to my [room](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=58738436203), dropped my bags and headed off downstairs and joined everyone for a [house raid](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=98738436203) and interesting time with their [cactus](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=19518436203).  After a few drinks and a few more friends, I headed off to bed.

The next morning, I had a great view of the [houses ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=69738436203)around the area and got to explore more of [Balmers](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=79738436203). 
Early morning, I signed up for paragliding, because I wasn't ready for ski
diving yet.  That was going to be tomorrow.  So, before the pickup time,
I explore the beautiful city of [Interlaken](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=44898436203).  There was a [grand casino.](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=60618436203)  (yes, those are clouds in the background, not my camera).  I also took an impromptu [nature hike.](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=68278436203) 
And soon, I was supposed to go paragliding, so I trotted back to Balmers. 
In the truck ride there, were the two girls I met in Paris, and 4 other girls
ready to be thrown into the air.  The ride there and back was simply [amazing](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=97898436203)!!!  It was truely a [winter wonderland](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=70378436203).  We took the cable car, to the top, above the clouds and had an even [better view](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=22378436203).  The 2 Parisien girls went first and the [3 other girls](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=19898436203) and [I waited](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=27809436203)
behind.  We walked around until we found a great slate of snow and I said,
"This is the 2nd time EVER that I've gotten to play with snow."  "REALLY?!!"
The girls said.  So, they [showed me ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=59898436203)how to make a [snow angel](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=92839436203).  I was [quite happy](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=58378436203).  I even got to [be a kid ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=38378436203)again.  Afterwards, the girls and I made [love notes ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=98378436203)to our admirers back home.  ([Jen](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=14839436203), [someone ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=44839436203)and [someone.](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=64839436203) 
I wish I was better with names.)  Two of the paraglider pilots came back
(one apparetly couldn't make it) and the other two girl went and Jen and
I stayed behind and had Swiss fondue for lunch.  About an hour later they
were ready again and we [trekked to the top ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=99378436203)of a high point and [Jen and I](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=40478436203) hyped ourselves up to do what others have told us to do for a long time...[go jump off a cliff](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=35839436203)!

And unfortunately, the weater did not permit us because there were [too many clouds ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=41909436203)which
would not allow for a safe landing...I asked them about waiting and they
said that it could take until tomorrow.  So, sadly, they walked back down. 
(I decided to roll down the mountain instead since I was still enjoy playing
in the snow.)  I then [enjoyed the ride back home ](http://www.ofoto.com/PhotoView.jsp?UV=911788206617_37130946203&US=0&collid=51268436203&photoid=21909436203)and chilled out with everyone else for another night at the hostel.

...  DANG!  I'm going to update this [tomorrow](http://discostu.radiopossibility.com/2001/11/23), because I want to make it to a Thanksgiving dinner!  Check back tomorrow for the rest!
