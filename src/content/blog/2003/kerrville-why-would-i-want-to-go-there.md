A while back, my good frirend Teel told me about this place called
Kerrville.  Being somewhat perceptive, I noticed that [Teel](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010196&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) seemed to like
this place a lot.  A LOT.  Like A LOT, A LOT.  No really.  More than that.

After finally letting my mind accept the fact that if I went to the
Kerrville Music and Wine festival, I would have to be camping the entire
weekend, possibly w/o showering.  I did enough of that back as a kid to know
that it's not my cup of tea, coffee, beer, or soy protein drink.  However,
feeling adventurous and curious, I decided to jump in.

I would say that I got more than I expected, but how can you really expect
something like Hippies named [Shaggy](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010165&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php), [sampling](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010142&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) and passing out numerous Texas
wines, [skinny dipping](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010131&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) and staying up till [5AM with about 20 other people](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010128&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php)
listening to lots of folk music and [comtemplating life?](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?name=News&file=article&sid=83)

Without a doubt, my favorite part of the weekend was being on wine duty,
where people would give someone else a dollar for a ticket and then give me
a ticket for ["a taste"](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010141&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) of wine.  The quotation marks are because as I
"tasted" the wine myself, my "tastes" got bigger.  For both me and the
ticket holders.  It was a win/win situation.  Except that I was on a red
wine table, and I strictly prefer sweet white wine.  But that just meant I
got to [make friends](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010158&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) with ... uh, um ... some vinyard that had a really good
traditional Reisling.  Man, I wish I remembered who it was.  Mind you, snake bites can be [pretty nasty.](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010162&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php)

[There was also a wedding!](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010184&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php)

By the time it was over, I was [sleep deprived](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010218&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php) to the point where I was [seeing things](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010220&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php), stinky and having problem
comprehending the English language.  All the signs of a [good time](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010177&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php).

-- Tommy now thinks that [hippies are cool](http://www.lastcoolnameleft.com/modules.php?set_albumName=album45&id=P1010174&op=modload&name=gallery&file=index&include=view_photo.php)!
