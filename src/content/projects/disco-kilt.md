I love kilts. I love disco. Two great tastes that go great together... right?

This project started as a joke and turned into a full build involving custom sewing, disco fabric, LED wiring, and several failed prototypes before the final DragonCon debut.

The result is one of the most delightfully extra things I've ever made.
