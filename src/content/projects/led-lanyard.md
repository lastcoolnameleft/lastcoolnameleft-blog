I got bored of regular conference lanyards, so I decided to make my own.

That led to experiments with LED strips, LEGO cases, Raspberry Pi controllers, field testing at conferences, and a whole lot of troubleshooting around power delivery and portability.

It is equal parts practical wearable and maximalist nerd accessory.
