Do you like pumpkins? I hate them. I want to see them smashed.

So I worked with friends to build a trebuchet, then built my own, and then the whole thing grew into a larger maker adventure from there.

This project sits at the intersection of hands-on engineering, woodworking, event building, and gleeful destruction.
